Why We Created This Website

As freshmen programmers embarking on our first hackathon, we recognized a common challenge faced by many students: finding the perfect spot to study or unwind with friends. With busy schedules and diverse preferences, navigating the myriad cafes and study locations can be overwhelming. We wanted to create a solution that not only helps students discover the best cafes and study spots but also fosters a sense of community among our fellow panthers. With our new creation we aim to save time, enhance productivity, and create a go-to resource for students. Ultimately, our goal is to make the journey of academic success more enjoyable and connected, one cafe at a time.
